Procurement Regions in India:

Dehydrated vegetable and fruit powders, as well as flakes, are sourced from a variety of regions across India, including Maharashtra, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, and Bihar.
